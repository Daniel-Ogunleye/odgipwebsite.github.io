About Us

We're the Jazz Cat Cafe!


Jazz cafe's for as long as we've known, have always been viewed as either; a gloomy underground or a fancy elitist gathering. But we don't quite agree with that.
So we changed up the aesthetic!

The Jazz Cat Cafe and it's founders aimed to create an environment where society could see the jazz world as wonderful and colorful as it is. No more gloomy and dark aesthetic to our jazz assemblies.

Joins us inside to become part of our growing community!
We hope to see you soon!
